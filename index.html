Measuring Vulnerability in the Sahel Region of Africa

INFORM is a world-wide, risk assessment for humanitarian crises and disasters. It helps to identify vulnerable areas and prioritize resource allocation for vulnerable peoples. The index ranks national administrative districts on a scale between 1 and 10, 10 being the most exposed to risk. The menu you allows you to explore the individual variables that go into creating indexes based on hazards, vulnerability, and lack of coping capacity all of which are combined to create the global INFORM index.
